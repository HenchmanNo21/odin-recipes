Russian Tea

Description

This Russian Tea Recipe from the 70's is a classic drink made with Tang, instant tea, and spices. Serve piping hot or over ice.

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 cups unsweetened instant tea powder
  • 2 cups Tang (or orange powder drink mix)
  • 1 tbsp lemonade powder mix (sweetened or unsweetened)
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cloves
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg (optional)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into a large plastic bag or Ziploc storage container.
  2. Mix ingredients well, either by stirring or shaking in a sealed container.
  3. Store in a sealed containter that keeps out moisture.
  4. Mix 3 to 4 tablespoons of the mix into a mug or glass of hot or cold water.
