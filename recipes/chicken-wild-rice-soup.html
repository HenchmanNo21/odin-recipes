Chicken Wild Rice Soup

Description

This chicken wild rice soup is delicious and easy to make using an instant pot.

Ingredients

  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 onion small, chopped
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 3 cups broth
  • 1 lb chicken thigh meat, cubed
  • 4 carrots, chopped
  • 3 stalks of celery, chopped
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tbsp fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 cup wild rice
  • 15 ounces coconut milk

Steps

  1. Press saute button and add coconut oil.
  2. Add in onion and saute for 3-4 minutes.
  3. Pour in broth and scrape bottom of the pan to remove any onion or garlic that may be stuck to the bottom.
  4. Add all other ingredients except for coconut milk.
  5. Close the lid, seal the vent, and press manual or pressure cook button and toggle to 35 minutes.
  6. After pressure cycle is complete, let natural release for 15 minutes, then release pressure.
  7. Stir in coconut milk and serve.
